This product is mainly to provide an exposure of the road safety analysis based on the statistics retrieved through various resources. It will benefit the government in order to emphasize on the biggest contributor to road accidents, for them to implement the necessary measures in order to make the roads in Malaysia less hazardous and dangerous to the people.

      Moreover, we hope to educate the people on the safety measures they could take up and adhere to, besides informing them on how the trends like on the accidents that happen over the years for them to be more alert on which is kind of road is more likely to be dangerous.
