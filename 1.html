Road Safety Suggestions

1. More speed trap cameras should be installed in order to force drivers to obey to the speed limits

2. Renovation of narrow parts of important roads and junctions need to be completed at the earliest

3. More reliable public transportation systems should be implemented in major and upcoming cities

4. Law enforcement officials should be outside of every clubs and pubs and breath tests should be implemented for every patron in order to avoid drunken driving

5. More law enforcement officials to monitor cars especially those who are driving over the limits

6. Law enforcement officials need to have a body camera to avoid bribery in any situation

7. More road safety campaigns should be organized in order to let the public knows on the safety measures
